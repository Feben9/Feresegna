About Us

Our Place

Feresegna Ethiopian Cuisine is more than just a restaurant, it’s a place to connect and create memories with loved ones. Our aim is to create a dining that feels authentic and welcoming to everyone who visits. we take great pride in serving traditional Ethiopian dishes made with only the freshest and finest ingredients.

A Few Words About Us

The finest Ethiopian food in Addis

We serve the freshest and finest ingredients, ensuring that every dish is made to perfection. Our menu has a wide variety of Ethiopian dishes, highlighting the country’s rich heritage. As soon as you enter our restaurant, you will be instantly transported to the captivating sights, sounds, and flavors of Ethiopia. Due to its authentic Ethiopian style, our restaurant can be referred as “The Little Ethiopia,” it captures the essence of the Ethiopian vibrant nation through its decor, menu, and warm hospitality.

Our Food Policy

Feresegna is committed to sourcing only the finest quality ingredients. We create dishes that are both unique and delicious. We also take great pride in keeping our kitchen clean and organized.
